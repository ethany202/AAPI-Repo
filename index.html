What is AAPI Connector?

Our goal is to provide individuals with information and resources to strengthen the AAPI community and provide an easy way to learn about and report hate crimes.

We find reputable resources for each of the three categories: aid, activism, and awareness for Asian American and Pacific Islander hate, especially as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic.
